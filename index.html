What Are We Talking About?

B-I-Trans Canada Highway

Hey, Canadians! Do you know your rights? Or are you more familiar with the rights of Americans?

Media and pop culture may give us the impression that we know the laws and rights that regulate the world around us, but our understanding of the regulatory and governing bodies in Canada might be assumptions from what we know about The US.

Join Karen and Alex as we sort through Canadian laws, regulations and rights, and review real Canadian cases that reference or even influence the law in question.

We’re not Un-American, we’re just not American.
